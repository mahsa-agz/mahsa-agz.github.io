CV-based ergonomic risk assessment

Computer-vision mobile app for ergonomic risk assessment. Validated 3D pose estimation against optical motion capture, computed automated REBA / RULA scores, and ran a usability study with non-experts. ICCs of 0.87 (REBA) and 0.92 (RULA); pilot SUS 80.5.
