About

I build multimodal AI tools that translate behavioural and sensor data into practical insights.

At 3R Innovation I work on PDSS, a clinical decision-support platform that helps clinicians identify ADHD-related patterns in children — combining cognitive task data with behavioural sensor signals into interpretable indicators clinicians can act on.

My master's at KAIST's Human Factors and Ergonomics Lab produced ErgoYar, a computer-vision mobile app for ergonomic assessment in SME workplaces — covering experiment design, motion-capture data collection, pose-estimation evaluation, mobile app build, and usability testing with non-experts.

Long term, I'd like to grow into technical program management — the space where research, engineering, product strategy and real-world user needs meet.
